About Youth Fellowships

Theme

"Flee the evil desires of youth and pursue righteousness, faith, love and peace along with those who call on the Lord out of pure heart" II Timothy 2:22.

Our youth fellowship exists :

  • To expose teenagers to God's love,
  • To equip them to exalt God,
  • To win souls for Christ,
  • To lay the foundation of their lives on Christ,
  • To taste the fruit of spirit always and
  • To lead a living example of Christ.

Timing: Every Sunday after the Service

Special Prayer:

Every Month third Saturday evening 5PM to 6PM
Every year Last Sunday Thanks giving prayer after Sunday Service.
