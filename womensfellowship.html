About Women’s Fellowship :

Our Women’s Fellowship is functioning and flourishing by the Grace of our Lord and by the able guidance of Respectful Mrs.Leela Manohari Joseph,Bishopamma President, Women’s fellowship CSI Diocese Madurai and Ramnad.

VISION :

To equip women to promote Unity, fellowship and justice among themselves, their families, Church and society

AIMS :

  • To develop and strengthen the spiritual thirst among women.
  • To make them understand their strength in leading and building the society in Christian spirit.
  • To be a role model for others in the married life and upbringing the children in His path.

Timing: Every Sunday after the Service

Special Prayer: Every Month third Friday evening 5PM to 6PM
