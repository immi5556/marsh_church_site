"It is good to praise the lord and make music to your name, with the music of ten-strings of
lute and the melody of the harp." Psalms 92:1-3.

"Music is the best solution to any problem when words fail, music speaks."

Our St. Paul's church choir is a small team of 39 members, which was strengthened recently by our pastor. We sing cheerful praises to the glory of our lord. We have practice every Saturday at 6.15 pm. We love to hear a choir and see faces of real people devoting co-operating themselves to a piece of music. We like the team work. It is our belief that everything we need to know about the world can be learned in a church choir. We get dearer to the lord and the spirit of the lord descend upon us through sacred music. Sometimes music is the only medicine to the heart and soul need.

May our lord help us to lift up our voice and sing with strength, spiritually praising god alone. Lord, Thank you for the music that brings our soul such joy "If any one is happy, sing praises god" James 5:13. God bless our choir.
