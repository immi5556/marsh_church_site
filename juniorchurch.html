About JC:

Here there are nearly 134 students of congregation,120 hostel students, 21 teachers, a superintendent and a convenor. We come under North Local Council of Dindigul Town from Madurai Ramnad Diocese.

Our order of service :

Our Sunday school begins at 8.45 am every week in Parish hall for all the children of congregation and hostel students. It starts from the age of 3 and up to Hr.sec.school level.The children are categorized as beginners( the children from pre.k.g to I std),primary(from II std to IV.std),juniors(from V std to VII std),inter(fromVIIIstd to Xstd),senior(from XI and XII std) and hostel students.

We have three sections of orders every week.

First section is GENERAL ASSEMBLY
Second section is Dept.WISE BIBLE STUDY
Third section is TEACHER'S DEVOTION

I.GENERAL ASSEMBLY

The dept.wise teachers are allowed to take this section every week.It starts with opening prayer, and then singing time with a music of contemporary worship songs and traditional hymns and chorus follows by a general sermon by using related pictures and sometime even projects pictures on large screens too.After that a memory verse related to sermon and a.verse from "Makilvikkum manna" is taught.The children are gifted and greeted for their academic excellence and for their birthdays.And this session ends with offerings and with some brief announcements.

II.Dept.WISE BIBLE Study

In this session the children are split into dept. Wise and a teacher is provided to them.One of the best way you can continue to grow in the relationship with God is by getting involved in such groups Here we follow CEEFI syllabus.2 quarters i.e 26 lessons per year is taught.In this class,they learn about God's love, discuss their experiences,pray for one another and rejoice in their answers to their prayers with their teachers.The children are provided with a hand book .They feel happy in doing their book exercises.And again they learn a new verse.The children have a cordial relationship with their teachers.

III.TEACHERS DEVOTION

After the disperse of children, the teachers have a meeting where they share their experiences.The senior Teachers used to give a small sermon & also testimonials are shared to glorify our Almighty.

MINISTRY WITH HOSTEL STUDENTS

Some teachers are allotted to part in encouraging the hostel students. They too have general assembly and a classiest small groups. Here the non Christian children are deeded with the bread of our lord.By this ministry we can see many volunteers and leaders have risen from here.

events: We have annual day program on this 4th week of Feb and we have CEEFI exam on July 2nd week

Aim : To teach Bible to children, and make them to grow in Christian faith.

Vision : Our children should reveal the love of God through their action & do the duties of real Christians.
